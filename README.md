# AI Jobs Analysis (2023â€“2025)

This project analyzes **AI job postings from 2023 to 2025** to understand how **demand, skills, and salaries** have evolved.
It explores:

* Where AI roles are most concentrated
* Which industries and companies drive the market
* What skills are most in demand
* How compensation varies by experience and company size

The study is organized into three main pillars, offering a complete view of the AI job landscape.

---

## I. Market Demand and Structure

This pillar focuses on **quantifying the overall market volume, segmentation, and growth trends.**

1. **Organizational Demand Drivers**
   Identify the dominant industries and top companies contributing the highest volume of AI job postings.

2. **Geographic Concentration**
   Determine the countries with the highest frequency of AI job openings.

3. **Role and Capacity Profile**
   Quantify the demand for the most common job titles, the primary experience levels being sought, and the prevailing employment types.

4. **Corporate Scale Demand**
   Analyze the distribution of job postings across different company sizes.

5. **Temporal Trends and Volatility**
   Examine the variation and growth trajectory of job postings over time (year, month, and month-over-year) to identify seasonal trends and market volatility.

---

## II. Talent Profile and Compensation

This pillar investigates **the requirements for talent and the financial incentives offered.**

1. **Skills and Tools Dominance**
   Determine the most required technical skills and preferred tools in the market, with a breakdown of these requirements for each major job position.

2. **Compensation Distribution**
   Analyze the distribution of minimum and maximum salaries to understand the typical earning potential and salary range within the AI field.

3. **Role-Specific Segmentation**
   For each common job title, identify:

   * The prevalent industry for the position
   * The primary experience level required
   * The dominant employment type offered

---

## III. Correlation and Flexibility

1. **Drivers of Salary and Volume**
   Analyze the correlation between key organizational variables (company size and required experience level) and financial metrics (average salary) or hiring volume (number of job postings).

2. **Negotiation Flexibility Analysis**
   Calculate the average salary negotiation range (`max salary - min salary`) for every major job position, and analyze its correlation with both experience level and company size to identify which market segments offer the most financial elasticity.
